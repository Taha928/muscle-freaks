Bulking Workout Plan

Build muscle mass and strength with this effective bulking workout plan. These exercises are designed to increase muscle size and improve strength over time.

Recommended Workouts

Chest Workout

Focus on building a strong, muscular chest with these exercises:

  • Bench Press - 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Chest Fly - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Push-Ups - 3 sets to failure

Back Workout

Strengthen your back muscles and improve your posture with these exercises:

  • Pull-Ups - 4 sets to failure
  • Deadlift - 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bent Over Row - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Leg Workout

Develop lower body strength and mass with these exercises:

  • Squats - 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Leg Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Leg Extensions - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Arm Workout

Increase arm size and definition with these focused exercises:

  • Bicep Curls - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Tricep Dips - 4 sets to failure
  • Hammer Curls - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep Pushdown - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
