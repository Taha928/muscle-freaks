Essential Nutrition

A balanced diet for bulking and overall health includes high-quality protein, healthy fats, and essential vitamins.

  • Eggs: Great source of protein and healthy fats, ideal for muscle repair and growth.
  • Chicken: High-protein meat that supports muscle building and tissue repair.
  • Lean Meat: Provides essential amino acids and iron for strength and energy.
  • Legumes: Packed with protein, fiber, and complex carbs for sustained energy.
